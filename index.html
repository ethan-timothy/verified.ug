Our Mission

Building trust in Uganda's digital space

Uganda Verified exists to create a reliable source of truth for individuals and organizations operating in Uganda. In an era where anyone can claim anything online, we bridge the trust gap by doing the hard work of verification — personally.

Every verified account has been reviewed through a direct video call and cross-referenced with official project registration documents or national identity records.

The Problem

Online trust in Uganda is broken

Scams, impersonation, and fraudulent organizations are rampant online. Donors lose money. Clients get defrauded. Communities are misled by fake NGOs and unregistered businesses.

There is no single, independent place to confirm that the person or organization you are dealing with is legitimate. Uganda Verified is that place.

The Process

How verification works

Every verification follows the same three-step process to ensure consistency and reliability.

1

Submit Application

Fill in the Get Verified form and book a time for a video call.

2

Video Call Review

We conduct a live video call with the applicant and review their official registration documents, national ID, or other relevant credentials.

3

Listed as Verified

Upon successful verification, the individual or organization is listed on Uganda Verified with a public profile and verification seal.

⚠️

We can make mistakes

While we do our best to thoroughly verify every applicant, no verification process is infallible. Documents can be forged. Circumstances change. If you believe a listing is incorrect or have evidence of fraud by a listed party, please contact us immediately so we can investigate and act accordingly.
